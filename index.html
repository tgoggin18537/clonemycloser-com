Three steps. Seven days.

We pull your top rep's actual SMS threads and email replies from your CRM (GHL, HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive). Minimum 500 SMS threads + 200 emails. No scripted role-plays. Real conversations only.

FAQ

The real questions.

What if my closer leaves the company?

The clone stays. We trained on their conversations, not on them. The bot keeps working leads in their writing style. Some clients actually use this as a retention insurance policy. You can also ask us to retire the clone if the rep specifically requests it when they leave.

Does it actually sound like my rep, or is it generic AI?

Sounds like your rep. We do not use generic AI personas. The bot is trained on your specific rep's actual SMS threads and email replies, with their signature phrases, their objection-handling patterns, and their closing language. We ship the eval scorecard before you go live. If your team doesn't agree the writing matches, we rebuild it or refund the setup (30-day Writing Match Guarantee).

Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Setup is one-time, retainer is month-to-month, no long-term contract. If you cancel within 30 days of go-live and we haven't matched your rep's voice, the setup fee is refunded. After 30 days, you can cancel the retainer anytime with 14 days notice. We hand off the bot and the data, you keep using it for 90 days as a transition window.

Is this TCPA-compliant?

Yes. The bot only replies to inbound conversations, doesn't initiate cold outbound unless you specifically opt in to that flow (and we handle A2P 10DLC registration on Twilio if you go that route). Every reply respects STOP, HELP, and unsubscribe keywords. We comply with state-level rules for two-party-consent jurisdictions automatically.

What CRMs do you support?

GoHighLevel (native), HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and Close.io out of the box. Other CRMs we integrate via API or webhook on Standard tier and up. If you have a CRM not listed, book a call and we'll confirm before you commit.

What if my rep doesn't have thousands of conversations?

Honest answer, you need at least 500 SMS threads or 200 emails for us to ship a credible clone. Below that the bot is generic, not cloned. On the discovery call we audit your CRM volume first and either confirm you have enough, scope a smaller deployment, or recommend you collect more data before we start. We won't take the contract if the data isn't there.

Who owns the AI clone after the contract?

You own the data and the prompt artifacts. We own the orchestration code (the Worker that runs everything). If we part ways, you keep the system prompt + training data + retrieval corpus and can self-host or take it to another vendor. We don't lock you in.
